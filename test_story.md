# The Little Star

Once upon a time, in a galaxy far, far away, there was a little star who was afraid of the dark.

One day, the little star decided to go on an adventure to find the light.

The little star met a friendly moon, who gave the star a special lantern to help it see in the dark.

With the lantern, the little star was no longer afraid of the dark and shined brightly for all to see.
